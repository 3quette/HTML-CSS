Adventure

You and your family will experience loads of fun when you vacation in Myrtle Beach! From popular attractions along the coast like Ripley’s Aquarium, Myrtle Waves, Family Kingdom, Go Ape, and the Skywheel – no two adventures are the same! Thrill-seekers can also enjoy activities such as helicopter rides, kayaking, and zip-lining, all just a short drive from our resorts.>

Golf

A little BIRDIE told me that Myrtle Beach is the best place to golf and that’s why it’s called the Golf Capital of the World. With nearly 80 courses to enjoy, the possibilities are endless. Experience courses with amazing views, tremendous greens, spectacular fairways, and even a little bit of history. The magnificent layouts carved out of the South Carolina coast will awe golfers with their beauty and put their skills to the test. No matter the type of golf getaway you are seeking, we have the best options for you!>

Discover the tastes of the beach!

Come discover the best eats along the coast! Whether your craving seafood or a classic southern dish, there are multiple places at the beach that are sure to satisfy your taste buds. With many local favorites and delicious hot spots, you won’t regret venturing out and trying something new. Check out Sea Captin’s House for an oceanfront dining experience or visit River City Cafe for unique twists on an American favorite. Whatever you are looking to experience, you will definitely find it here at the beach!
